Our History

Bountiful Foods was established in 1989 by Freidgrik McGoth. Freidgrik was born on a farm in Montana. His father was a teacher at a grade school, so in order to help his father pay the bills he would sell the fresh produce at the local market. The year 1989 Freidgrik's dad lost his job, so he and his brother would go to each of their neighbors and for any of the elderly who couldn't, they would take and sell it for them. He and his brother also started advertising their fresh produce online and they established Bountiful Foods.

Where We Get Our Fresh Produce

We strive to provide our customers with the freshest foods from across the globe. We make sure that our bananas are straight from Africa, our tomatoes come from India, and we make sure that our pineapple is shipped from Costa Rica. We strive to ship you products that use minimal chemicals and pesticides in order to keep it fresh.
